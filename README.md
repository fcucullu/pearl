# Pearl 🐚

**Period & cycle tracker — know your body, feel your best.**

## Problem
Most period trackers are either too complex, data-hungry, or lack meaningful insights about how each cycle phase affects daily life.

## Solution
Pearl is a simple, beautiful period tracker that predicts your cycle, shows your current phase with personalized recommendations, and optionally keeps your partner informed with helpful tips.

## Who is it for?
Women who want a straightforward way to track their cycle and understand their body better, with the option to share phase info with their partner.

## Features
- **Smart predictions** — Log your periods and Pearl learns your unique cycle length
- **4-phase calendar** — Color-coded calendar showing menstrual, follicular, ovulation, and luteal phases
- **Phase recommendations** — Daily self-care tips based on your current phase
- **Symptom tracking** — Optional mood, energy, pain, and other symptom logging
- **Partner notifications** — Email your partner when you change phases with tips on how to support you
- **Stats & trends** — Average cycle length, period duration, and cycle count

## Tech Stack
- Next.js (App Router)
- Supabase (Auth + Database)
- Tailwind CSS v4
- Vercel (Hosting)
